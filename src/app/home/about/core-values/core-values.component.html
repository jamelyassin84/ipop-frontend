Core Values

We the constituents of the Provincial Population Office (PPO) , commit ourselves to the following core values and endeavours to LIVE IT :


Love of Service

Love of fellowmen expressed through service beyond self is the over-arching principle that guides all other actions and endeavours.


Innovation

We believe that the creation of new ideas ad initiative in the context of our cultural heritage, is the noblest response to the challenge of change in our communities.


Vigor and Enthusiasm

will be our most profound expression of our commitment to our vision, mission, and goals.


Excellence

Our efforts and actions will be guided by the highest standards of quality and excellence.


Integrity

We uphold the principle of truth and honesty, realizing that in our dealings, we are accountable to each other and to our clients, partners, and stakeholders.


Team Work

Given our diverse talent and skills, we will work together to achieve PPO goals
